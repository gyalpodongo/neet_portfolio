Week 9 22.03

This week we presented our findings in front of other engineers and implemented some of their feedback.

So now there will be two sliders!

One of the sliders has holes and gaps of 3/8 inches each.

The second slider will have a separation between the holes of 5/8 inches whereas the holes width is 3/8 inches.

The reason for this was so that we can make sure both pieces can slide overeach other smoothly as none of the holes fit into the other pieces from the other slider!



This is the first slider that we have, the original one.



This is how the process for the second slide started by putting it on the mill.
