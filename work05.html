Week 5 22.03

To narrow our options, we decided to choose some desired meals we want to cook using our product and start working from this point.

The dishes we decided to cook are

  • Chicken Kabobs
  • Tex Mex Quinoa
  • Grilled Tomatoes with rice

As part of the cooking team, we have given some instructions to the energy storage team about what are some of our requirements.

We need temperatures ranging from ~200 F - ~450 F.

We also came up with a hot dog cart like desing for our project, which we called R.I.C.K. (Renewable Independent Cooking Kitchen).

We pinned this up in front of the class to see what they think!

More details can be found in our presentation slides.

Come back next week to see how our class project has progressed!
